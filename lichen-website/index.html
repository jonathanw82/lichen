The Story
You didn't start growing to babysit a thermometer.

You started because there is something quietly extraordinary about coaxing life from a seed. The first green shoot breaking through dark soil. The smell of a greenhouse in full growth. The satisfaction of a harvest you raised with your own hands.

But greenhouses are unforgiving. A warm afternoon that tips into a scorching afternoon. A vent left closed on a humid night. A crop that looked fine on Tuesday and was lost by Thursday. Every grower knows that feeling. The one that arrives too late.

"What if your greenhouse could think for itself — so you never had to worry about it again?"

That question is exactly why Lichen exists.

What Is Lichen
The intelligent system that never sleeps.

Lichen is a complete greenhouse intelligence platform. It lives on your network, connects wirelessly to sensor nodes placed throughout your growing spaces, and monitors everything — temperature, humidity, ventilation, light levels, soil moisture — around the clock, in real time.

And it doesn't just watch. It acts.

When temperatures climb too high, Lichen opens your vents — automatically, precisely, without you needing to be there. When conditions return to the right range, it closes them again. It adapts to your greenhouse, learns its rhythms, and keeps your plants in the environment they need to thrive.

All of it visible, at a glance, on any phone, tablet, or computer. Wherever you are.
